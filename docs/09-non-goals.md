# Non-goals

Do not build these until explicitly scheduled:

- full ERP;
- CNC/machine export;
- stock and purchasing;
- official CE/DoP/Uw claims;
- invoicing;
- accounting/e-Factura integration;
- advanced production optimization;
- complex irregular shapes;
- supplier marketplace;
- AI pricing recommendations.

The first product must win on mobile quote speed, configurable catalogs, reliable calculations, saved offers, and professional PDFs.
