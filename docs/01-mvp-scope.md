# MVP scope

The MVP covers the complete commercial quote loop:

1. Authentication and tenant setup.
2. Company settings and user preferences.
3. Customer and project records.
4. Door/window/custom quote items.
5. Catalog admin for profiles, glass, hardware, colors, accessories, services, taxes, and price lists.
6. Calculation engine with material cost, commercial addition, taxes, discounts, manual overrides, glass cuts, and profile linear meters.
7. PDF offer generation.
8. Saved offers with filters.
9. Quote versions and revisions.

Out of scope for MVP:
- full production ERP;
- CNC export;
- cut optimization;
- stock management;
- official CE/DoP documents;
- invoicing;
- accounting integrations;
- advanced irregular shapes.
